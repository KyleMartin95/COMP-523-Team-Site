How To Use

ChickenBus operates on a web-based portal, and can be accessed freely. A mobile application is currently being developed to be deployed by the end of 2017. In order to access bus route information simply enter your current location and your desired location in the search panel along with when you would like the departure to take place.

Optimal routes will appear next the search panel in the map interface.

FAQS

  • What if nothing is returned when I enter my origin, destination, and departure time? - Then it is likely there is not a route within your specified time frame, or radius. It is equally likely that we do not have any bus route information for your current area.
